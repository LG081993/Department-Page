Introduction

A descriptive essay is a type of academic essay wherein the writer will provide a detailed description of a place, person, experience, emotion, or a particular process. It is the kind of essay wherein the writer can approach it by providing information basing on his or her subjective perception. Universities often give this assignment to the students to let them enhance their essay writing skills.

Eligibility Criteria for Students and Selection Procedure for the Free Coaching :

i. In order to be considered for admission into the desired courses/recruitment examinations, candidates must have achieved the required minimum percentage of marks in the qualifying examination.

ii. The Scheme is open to only Scheduled Caste students. University shall obtain/verify requisite caste certificate from the concerned candidate / student/ issued by the competent authorities in his/her respective States/UTs or as the case may be.

iii. The coaching benefits under the scheme may only be utilized by a student once regardless of the number of chances that he/she may be entitled to in a particular Competitive Examination. The student must submit to the University an affidavit stating that he/she has not received any monetary benefit from any of the other scheme by Government of India, State/UTs or any funding agency.

iv. The candidates enrolled under this scheme shall have to attend all the classes. In case of remaining absent for more than 15 days without any valid reason, or leaving the coaching midway without prior approval of the competent authority, the total expenditure incurred on the candidate will be recovered from the University/student/candidate concerned.

v. A total of 100 (one hundred) seats is available for coaching at the Centre out of which 33% seats are meant for eligible female candidates from the SC category. In case sufficient number of eligible female candidates does not apply, the University may allocate the vacant seats to the male/transgender candidates from the SC category.

vi. The students are selected on the basis of merit through an Entrance Test to be conducted by the University.

vii. The University maintains attendance registers for all candidates enrolled for the coaching classes as well as subject-wise results of practice tests, or any other evaluation.

Entrance Examination :

An Entrance Examination for screening candidates for the coaching will be held in the month of August 2022. The date for the Examination will be notified soon.

The candidates are advised to go through the information relating to the Entrance Examination given in the following link.

It may be noted that the Syllabus for the Entrance Examination will be same as that of the UPSC Civil Services (Preliminary) Examination. However, there will be only ONE PAPER of 100 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) for the Entrance Examination.
